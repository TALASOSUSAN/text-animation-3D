I love technology because it’s the triumph of the human intellect.
The more I study and understand technology,
the more I feel that nothing in the universe is out of reach.
There’s even a great rush of power that comes from
coding your first simple “Hello World” program.
Getting it to run successfully,
seeing those two words on the screen and knowing that
YOU put them there just makes you think of the endless possibilities…
